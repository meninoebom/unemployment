Unemployment: Recap

Level 1: Recognize

Distinguish between employment, the different types of unemployment (frictional, structural, and cyclical), the labor force, the non-labor force, and the adult population.

Level 2: Calculate

Calculate the unemployment, employment, and labor force participation rates, along with the natural rate of unemployment using simulated data.

Level 3: Analyze

Explain and measure the unemployment, employment, and labor force participation rates, along with the natural rate of unemployment, using real national, state, and regional data.

Level 4: Compare

Compare the different measures of the United States unemployment, employment, and labor force participation rates over time.
